A set of ingredients for Russian salad is classical. Nothing extra will be added.
Peel and chop finely the onion.
Soak raw onions in boiling water for 5 minutes. Drain the liquid and use the softened onions in salad. Thanks to soak the onion becomes soft, it isn’t bitter or crispy.
Cut boiled meat into small cubes (you can substitute the meat with cooked sausage).
Russian salad is ready!
